The Batman

Batman, also known as the Dark Knight or the Caped Crusader is the primary protagonist and guardian of the city of Gotham.
Batman, whose real identity is Bruce Wayne is the billionaire playboy heir of the Wayne family fortune, however Gotham's greatness
is overshadowed by the dark underbelly of crime filled with notorious villains such as The Penguin, the Falcone crime family, The Riddler and the
crown prince of insanity himself, The Joker. Batman and Bruce Wayne are cared for by the Wayne family's loyal butler, Alfred as well as Wayne Enterprises
business manager, Lucius Fox. The Batman universe was revitalized through the Dark Knight trilogy, directed by Christopher Nolan.
